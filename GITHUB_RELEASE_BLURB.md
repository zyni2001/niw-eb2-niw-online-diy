## Release Blurb

`EB-2 NIW DIY Skill` is a reusable Codex skill for building a self-filed EB-2 National Interest Waiver I-140 packet, especially for USCIS online filing. It covers Dhanasar-based evidence mapping, NIW petition-letter drafting, recommendation-letter drafting, USCIS upload-bucket organization, packet naming, PDF assembly, and normalization of screenshot-heavy evidence into readable letter-size uploads. The skill is designed to help applicants produce a clearer, more defensible NIW submission workflow while keeping government policy evidence, third-party validation, and practical packet organization aligned.
